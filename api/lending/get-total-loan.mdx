---
title: 'Customers Total Loan'
api: 'GET https://finlend.fincode.software/api/v1/services/user/get-total/{customerId}'
description: 'Retrieves the total borrowed loan amount for a specific customer.'
---

Gets the total amount of loans borrowed by the customer identified by the `customerId`.

<RequestExample>
  ```bash cURL
  curl --request GET 'https://finlend.fincode.software/api/v1/services/user/get-total/8ecca811-607f-44e1-a57e-cd856c50437d' \
  --header 'Authorization: Bearer <token>'
  ```
</RequestExample>
